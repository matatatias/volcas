volca global settings cheat sheet

To access global settings, turn on your volca while holding FUNC button

  • Volca Keys and Modular don't have a display.
  • Volca Modular doesn't have settings 6 and 7
Led
unlit/lit		 Display

Step 1
Auto power-off function 		Disabled AP.oF AutoP OFF APO off
Enabled AP.on AutoP On APO on

Battery type selection 		Alkaline bt.AL bAtt AL BAT alka
Nickel-metal hydride bt.nH bAtt nH BAT nick
Step 3
Sync Out polarity 		Rise So.Hi SyncOt HI SYO high
Fall So.Lo SyncOt Lo SYO low
Step 4
Sync In polarity 		Rise SI.HI SyncIn HI SYI high
Fall SI.Lo SyncIn Lo SYI low
Step 5
Tempo range settings 		Narrow (56-240) tP.nr TEmPo nAr TMP narr
Full (10-600) tP.FL TEmPo FuL TMP full
Step 6
MIDI Clock Src 		Internal CL.In MdCLK Int MCL int
Auto CL.At MdCLK Aut MCL auto
Step 7
MIDI RX ShortMessage 		Off St.oF MdShrt OF MST off
On St.on MdShrt On MST on
Step 8
Sync input/output unit 		Once every 2 steps StP.2 SyncStp 2 SYC 2stp
Once a step StP.1 SyncStp 1 SYC 1stp
